Inventory Management Automation System

The Hailakandi Polytechnic Inventory Management Automation System provides a centralized digital platform for managing institutional assets, laboratory equipment, and departmental stock in an organized and efficient manner. This system is designed to simplify inventory control and improve operational workflow.

The dashboard enables real-time stock monitoring, department-wise tracking, and structured data management. Administrators can add items, update inventory, view stock details, and maintain accurate records across all departments.

This platform enhances transparency, reduces manual workload, and ensures effective utilization of institutional resources through a modern automated inventory management approach.
